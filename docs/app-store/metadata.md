## English

- Name: AudioInputLocker
- Subtitle: Lock your Mac's input device
- Category: Utilities
- Price: Free
- Description:

AudioInputLocker is a small macOS menu bar utility for managing the system
default audio input device.

It gives microphones and other input devices a native-feeling Sound menu of
their own. Pick an input device from the menu, adjust input volume when the
device supports it, and lock your preferred device so macOS or another app
cannot quietly switch it away.

AudioInputLocker works locally on your Mac and does not collect data.

- Keywords: audio,input,microphone,menu bar,sound,AirPods,utility
